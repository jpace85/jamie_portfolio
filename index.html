What I do

My Role

I currently work as a Web Developer for The Ticket Factory, the UK’s leading ticketing agent and most successful venue box office for the NEC, Resorts World Arena and Utilita Arena Birmingham.

My Clients

Our client’s that include the RHS, LTA and the BBC, will come to us to create a branded and often bespoke white label website to suit the needs of customers wishing to buy tickets for their exhibitions and events.

My Responsibilities

It is my responsibility to create these websites, maintain them during the sales period and adapt or improve them depending on the client’s needs. We can often have 20+ white label websites that are “live” and selling tickets, which may need alterations or amendments at any time.
